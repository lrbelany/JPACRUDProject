One thing that always stands out in my mind when I see this variant though, is what happened the first time I ever saw one. I was playing only my 4th or 5th BTech game with a group I had joined in Grand Rapids. We played a Mercenary Company, and our Commander piloted a captured Timberwolf D. I was controlling a mixed Heavy/Assault Lance comprised of a 3F Stalker, a Pillager, a Guillotine, and something else I can't remember anymore. For this day's gaming session we sat down and started walking across the map. Next thing we know, around from behind the huge mountain in the middle stalks a Dire Wolf Prime. Around the other side the rest of an Assault Binary of Jade Falcon mechs. Opening round of shooting: Commander lifts his T-Wolf's left arm, BOOM! Headcaps the Dire Wolf. The battle for the Clanners went downhill from there, and our Commander upgraded to a Dire Wolf. Ahh.. fond memories. Because of that this Mech is always special to me.
